Welcome to the Music Production Masterclass!

In this first module, we will explore the world of Digital Audio Workstations (DAWs).
A DAW is the heart of your studio. Whether you use Ableton Live, FL Studio, Logic Pro, or Pro Tools, the fundamental concepts remain the same.

Key topics covered:
1. Choosing the right DAW for your workflow.
2. Understanding the interface (Timeline vs. Session view).
3. Setting up your audio interface and MIDI controllers.
4. Basic recording and editing techniques.
