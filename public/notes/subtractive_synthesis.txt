Subtractive synthesis is the foundation of most modern electronic sounds.
By starting with a rich waveform and "subtracting" frequencies with a filter, we can create everything from deep bass to bright leads.

Key concepts in this module:
1. Oscillators (Saw, Square, Sine, Triangle).
2. Filters (Low-pass, High-pass, Band-pass).
3. Envelopes (ADSR: Attack, Decay, Sustain, Release).
4. LFOs (Low-Frequency Oscillators) for modulation.

Experiment with these parameters to find your unique sound!
