Understanding Signal Flow is one of the most important concepts in audio engineering.
It's the path that audio takes from its source (microphone, instrument, or software) to its destination (speakers, headphones, or a recording).

In this module, we will cover:
1. Input vs. Output.
2. Gain Staging: Why it matters and how to do it right.
3. Inserts vs. Sends: When to use each.
4. Routing audio between tracks and buses.

Mastering signal flow will save you hours of troubleshooting later!
