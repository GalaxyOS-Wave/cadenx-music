Equalization (EQ) is the most powerful tool in your mixing arsenal.
By adjusting the balance between frequency components, we can create space and clarity in our mixes.

In this module, we will explore:
1. Parametric vs. Graphic EQ.
2. Low-cut/High-cut filters for cleaning up tracks.
3. Boosting vs. Cutting: Why cutting is usually better.
4. Identifying and fixing frequency clashes between instruments.

A clean mix starts with a good EQ strategy!
