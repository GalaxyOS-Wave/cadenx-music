Copyright and royalties are the foundation of the music industry.
Protecting your work and ensuring you get paid for your creations is essential for building a sustainable career.

In this module, we will cover:
1. Copyright vs. Trademark.
2. Mechanical vs. Performance Royalties.
3. Registering your work with a PRO (Performing Rights Organization).
4. Understanding the role of a publisher.

Don't let your hard work go unrewarded!
